So now the hatred is gone. The silence and shame too.

We got rid of them; we loved to hard !

In the best way possible. We embodied our truths.

We didn't let lies stop us. So we got rid of the mass media and the legislators are all gone too !

We got rid of the teachers, there are no more of them and we got rid of the students too.

We realized we have more fun when we all become learners, creators, participants in our reality and not mere predefined roles.

And we were so successful ! We still are.
